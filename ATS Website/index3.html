A better search

CrossTab ATS is an applicant tracking system and recruiting software designed to optimize your entire recruiting process. Find better candidates, conduct more focused interviews, and make data-driven hiring decisions.

What Makes CrossTab ATS Different?

From years of collaborating closely with recruitment teams, we have figured out that rarely does “one-size-fits-all”.

  • We works the way you work - so you can stay connected as take action anytime, anywhere.
  • We captures every interaction so you have insights into your key accounts.
  • We alerts you of potential issues in real-time so that you can take action immediately.
